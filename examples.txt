# Plural

> The ox-s are brown.
< The oxen are brown.

> The ox-s is-s brown.
< The oxen are brown.

> Ox-s, fish-s, cactus-s & mouse-s
< Oxen, fish, cacti & mice


# Past tense

> The ox is-ed brown.
< The ox was brown.

> The ox-s is-s-ed brown.
< The oxen were brown.

> He like-ed pie.
< He liked pie.
